# My PC Building Checklist

A practical starting checklist before I begin a new build:

- Confirm use case, budget, and power target.
- Match CPU, memory, and motherboard socket requirements.
- Verify thermal budget and airflow plan.
- Run memory, storage, and stress checks before final installation.

Keep this list handy for clean, repeatable builds and future maintenance.
